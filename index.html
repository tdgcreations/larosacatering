Meet Isabel Paredes

Isabel specializes in fine Mexican cuisine and serves clients throughout San Diego.

She studied at the California Culinary Arts Institute and learned to cook from a young age alongside her mother. Today, she carries those lessons forward through thoughtful menus, generous hospitality, and food designed to make every gathering feel special.

Her goal is simple: to bring every guest a taste of a refined culinary experience while preserving the warmth and soul of family-inspired Mexican cooking.

— Isabel Paredes
